About

I'm Michelle Rascati, hence the MiRa pseudonym.

Born and raised in Austin, TX, I went to the University of Texas for BS degrees in Astronomy and Physics. My research involved analyzing telescope data of astronomical objects. If you'd like to learn more about that, check out our paper or contact me.

As I picked up some programming skills for my research, I decided I'd like to pursue some kind of coding full time (with the bonus of being more practical than becoming an astronomer). I stayed in Austin for a while, navigating my way around SQL and relational databases. Once I moved to Seattle with my now fiance, I went all in and completed a Python bootcamp at Code Fellows. Since then, I've been freelancing for some start-ups around the PNW.

If I'm not sitting at a desk, you'll likely find me flailing at a bouldering gym, stretching my legs in yoga, soaking up what little rays we get in Seattle, or searching for some good grub.
